## 🧩 Perspective

Two ideas strongly influence how I think about AI and robotics:

> “The biggest breakthroughs in AI have come from methods that scale with computation.”  
> — Rich Sutton, *The Bitter Lesson*

and

> *Moravec’s Paradox*:  
> High-level reasoning is often easier for machines than low-level perception and control.

Together, they point toward embodied learning:
**systems that act in and respond to physical environments**, not just produce outputs.
